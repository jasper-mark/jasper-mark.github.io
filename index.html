About

I hold a bachelor's in Exercise and Sport Science from The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, NC and a Ph.D. in Human Movement Science focusing on computational neuroscience techniques and neuromotor control from The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill School of Medicine, NC.

The focus of my research is to develop computational methods for modeling nonstationary dynamics in complex systems and relating those dynamics to human behavior and clinical outcomes.
